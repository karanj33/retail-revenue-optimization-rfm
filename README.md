

#  Retail Revenue Optimization & Customer Segmentation using RFM

##  Business Problem

Retail businesses often lose revenue due to **poor customer retention and lack of segmentation**.
This project focuses on identifying **where revenue is leaking** and how to recover it using data.

---

##  Objective

* Identify **high-value, at-risk, and lost customers**
* Analyze **purchase behavior and revenue trends**
* Detect **churn patterns and seasonality**
* Provide **data-driven strategies to improve retention and revenue**

---

##  Tools Used

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* Power BI

---

##  Approach

* Cleaned and processed transactional data
* Created **RFM metrics (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)**
* Applied **quantile-based scoring (`pd.qcut`)** for segmentation
* Built an interactive **Power BI dashboard** for business insights

---

## Key Insights

* **47% of customers are At-Risk or Lost** → major revenue leakage
* Revenue is heavily dependent on **Q4**, creating unstable cash flow
* **UK contributes ~80% of total revenue** → geographic concentration risk
* Identified **high-frequency, high-volume buyers in off-season**, indicating a hidden **B2B/wholesale segment**

---

## Business Impact

* Highlighted **customer churn risk** and retention gaps
* Identified opportunities for **targeted reactivation campaigns**
* Revealed potential for **subscription/wholesale revenue streams**
* Enabled better decision-making through **interactive dashboard insights**

---

##  Dashboard Highlights

* Total Revenue: **$8.89M**
* Customer Segmentation (RFM)
* Monthly Revenue Trends
* Average Order Value (AOV)
